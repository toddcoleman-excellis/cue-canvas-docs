Notifications

The Notifications system surfaces in-app alerts to users via a timeline-style panel. It consists of sub-components — the Notification Block, Timeline Connector, Date and Time, Notification Indicator, and Badge — that compose into a full Notification Panel.

Notification Panel

The full panel renders a scrollable list of Notification Blocks in a card container. Unread items display a filled blue timeline icon; read items show an empty circle. The timeline connector line threads continuously between all items.

Sub-components

The building blocks that compose a Notification Block and Panel.

Timeline Circle and Connector

A vertical connector line threaded through a circle icon. The circle fills blue (bg-accent-brand) when the notification is unread. The top and bottom connector segments link continuously between stacked blocks.

Date and Time

Displays the notification timestamp. Date, bullet separator, and time render inline using the 11pt body style in content-body-subdued.

01/01/2025 • 6:00 PM

Notification Indicator

A small dot used in the app shell topbar or navigation to signal unread notifications. The accent-brand fill with a primary-brand border creates a visible call-out on dark header backgrounds.

9

Notifications Badge with Number

A circular badge showing unread count, positioned on the notification bell icon in the app topbar. Uses a white background with an accent-brand border and primary-brand text.
