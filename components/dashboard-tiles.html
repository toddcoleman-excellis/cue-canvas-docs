Dashboard Tiles

Navigation tiles used on the CUE Back Office dashboard to route users into product modules. Each tile has a 48px icon, a bold title, and an optional description. Arranged in responsive grids grouped by product area. Two size variants (Default and Smaller) and four states (Default, Hover, Disabled, Disabled Hover).

States

Four states. Hover lifts the shadow from Shadow/2 to Shadow/8 and darkens the border. Disabled greys out the icon, title, and description.
